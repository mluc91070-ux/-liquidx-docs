# Glossary

## Allocation

Capital assigned to a LiquidX pack, pool, vault, or internal liquidity route.

## Allocation tier

A pack level that defines how much USDT a user chooses to allocate.

## Capital at risk

The user's allocated funds may lose value or be lost entirely.

## Community Vault

A shared allocation structure where user deposits are grouped together so smaller users can participate together in liquidity activity that would normally require larger capital.

## Counterparty

Another party involved in execution, settlement, custody, routing, liquidity activity, or operational services.

## Dashboard

The LiquidX tracking interface inside the Telegram app. It may show deposits, pack selection, balance, projected performance, rewards, referral rewards, activity, and withdrawal status.

## Fee-based liquidity

Liquidity activity where performance may come from fees linked to capital availability, routing, execution, settlement, or liquidity access.

## Liquidity

The ability to buy or sell an asset without causing a major price move.

## Liquidity Captain

A LiquidX KOL or ambassador who educates users, supports onboarding, grows communities, and may participate in referral or campaign-based rewards.

## Liquidity provider

A person or entity that supplies capital so markets, routes, pools, or desks can function. In LiquidX, users act as simplified liquidity providers through packs while LiquidX manages the allocation layer.

## Liquidity pool

A structure that groups capital so it can support selected liquidity needs such as OTC flow, market liquidity, stablecoin movement, market-making activity, private execution, or internal balancing.

## Market making

Providing buy and sell liquidity to a market, often by quoting both sides and managing spread, inventory, and execution risk.

## Monthly target

An illustrative reference figure that may describe expected or projected performance. It is not a guaranteed return.

## OTC

Over-the-counter. A transaction that happens directly between parties instead of through a public exchange order book.

## Pack

A simple allocation tier in LiquidX, such as the Starter Pack, Builder Pack, Growth Pack, Operator Pack, Prime Pack, or Elite Pack.

## Projection

An estimate shown for transparency. A projection is not a promise, contract, or guarantee.

## Referral reward

A potential reward based on real invited users and real deposit activity, subject to program rules and checks.

## Reward

A potential dashboard credit or user benefit calculated from eligible liquidity activity, fees, vault performance, referral activity, or product rules. Rewards are variable and not guaranteed.

## Spread capture

Potential performance from the difference between buy and sell prices or execution routes. Spread capture can be reduced or reversed by volatility, poor execution, or market stress.

## Stablecoin risk

Risk related to USDT or other stablecoins, including depeg, issuer, regulatory, liquidity, freeze, network, or exchange withdrawal risk.

## Vault

An allocation structure that may group deposits, route funds into selected strategies, track performance, support rewards, and manage withdrawal cycles.

## Withdrawal cycle

The timing or process through which allocated capital may become available for withdrawal, depending on product rules, vault status, liquidity availability, processing windows, and security checks.
