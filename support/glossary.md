# Glossary

## Allocation

Capital deposited by a member and assigned to LiquidX liquidity strategies.

## AUM

Assets under management or assets allocated through the platform. This number should only be published if verified.

## Capital at risk

The possibility that allocated capital may decrease or be lost.

## Counterparty

A third party involved in a transaction, trade, settlement, or liquidity operation.

## Desk

The operational team or infrastructure responsible for liquidity execution and routing.

## Market making

Providing buy and sell liquidity to a market.

## OTC

Over-the-counter trading, where trades are executed directly between parties rather than through a public order book.

## Qualified invite

A referred member who completes the required deposit conditions.

## Reference monthly rate

A variable monthly figure based on the LiquidX methodology. It is not guaranteed.

## Stablecoin

A crypto asset designed to track the value of another asset, often the US dollar.

## USDT

A widely used USD-denominated stablecoin.

## Withdrawal window

The period or conditions under which a withdrawal request can be submitted or processed.
