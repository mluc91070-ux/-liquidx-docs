# LiquidX

## Liquidity for Everyone

LiquidX is a Telegram-native liquidity access app for users who want simple exposure to liquidity-based opportunities without trading manually.

Users can open the LiquidX Telegram Mini App, deposit USDT, choose a liquidity pack, track their dashboard, invite others through referral, and request withdrawals from inside Telegram.

LiquidX is not a casino, signal group, or guaranteed-return product. It is designed as an education-first access layer for liquidity activity such as OTC flow, crypto market liquidity, stablecoin flows, market depth opportunities, commodities-related market activity, and vault-based capital allocation.

## What you can do

With LiquidX, eligible users can:

* Deposit USDT through the official Telegram app.
* Choose a pack starting from $25.
* Let capital enter internal liquidity routes, pools, or vaults.
* Track balance, activity, projected performance, and referral rewards.
* Request withdrawals according to product rules, lock periods, processing windows, and liquidity conditions.
* Invite real users and earn referral rewards based on real deposit activity.
