# Security & Custody

Skeptical money asks where it sleeps.

This section explains the LiquidX security model in plain language.

## Custody model

LiquidX allocations are held in USDT and managed by the desk according to the platform's operational model.

LiquidX states clearly:

* Which assets are accepted.
* Which networks are supported.
* Where deposits are sent.
* Who controls operational wallets.
* How withdrawals are processed.
* What controls exist before funds move.

LiquidX does not use vague claims like "your funds are 100% safe."

No crypto system can honestly make that claim.

## Account protection

LiquidX account protection may include:

* Telegram account verification.
* Two-factor confirmation for sensitive actions.
* Withdrawal address confirmation.
* Manual review for unusual activity.
* Anti-phishing warnings.
* Suspicious activity monitoring.

## Withdrawal address safety

Before withdrawing, always verify:

* Network.
* Address.
* Amount.
* Destination wallet.
* Any memo or tag requirement.
* Final confirmation screen.

Crypto withdrawals are usually irreversible.
